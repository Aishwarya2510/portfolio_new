# First Value — Notion Onboarding Audit

A standalone, portfolio-ready onboarding audit and redesign concept for Notion.

## Included
- Executive product diagnosis and focused persona
- Current-state and redesigned onboarding flows
- Five-dimension scoring matrix
- Three psychologically grounded drop-off risks
- Three prioritized product recommendations
- Interactive first-session prototype
- Activation metric tree and analytics event taxonomy
- Onboarding orchestration architecture
- Directional impact calculator
- A/B test design, delivery roadmap, tradeoffs, and safeguards
- Interview narrative and evidence register

## Run
Open `index.html` in any current browser. No build tools or external libraries are required.

## Research boundaries
This is an independent portfolio case study. Official product facts and public signals are linked in the source section. Flow scores, activation assumptions, impact estimates, and experiment thresholds are product hypotheses, not Notion results.
